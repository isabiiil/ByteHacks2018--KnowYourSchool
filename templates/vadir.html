2014 - 2015 VADIR Incidents-3

Summary

VADIR stands for Violent And Disruptive Incidents Report. The dataset contains statistics for all different types of incidents, for every public school.

Columns

Column Name Definition
County The borough in which the school is located. In this dataset, the options are Bronx, Kings, New York, Queens, and Richmond.
District The geographical district that the school is located in.
School Name The official name of the school.
Grade Organization The grades that the school serves: elementary, middle, junior high, or senior high.
Enrollment The number of students attending the school.
